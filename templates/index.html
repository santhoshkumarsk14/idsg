About IDSG

Established in Singapore in 2013, IDSG has a 200 square meter local workshop, equipped with CNC, milling, lathe, drilling, cutting, laser, bending, tapping machinery, etc. We are able to provide all kinds of engineering service for you promptly.

  • We provide Design & Consultation service since the early stage of your idea conceptualization
  • We provide Simulation & Rapid Prototyping service to verify your technologies
  • We provide Customization service on your component level & system level
  • We provide Scale-Up service from laboratory to industry
  • We provide Production service for the final push to the market

We serve both Academic and Industrial market, such as universities, research centers, institutes and MNCs & SMEs.

Our business focuses are mainly environmental engineering, precision engineering, civil engineering, thermal & fluidic dynamics, automation & robotics, etc.

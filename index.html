About Us

Founded in Singapore in 2013, we are a premier engineering company offering a comprehensive suite of services that drive innovation and excellence across industries. With a state-of-the-art 200-square-meter workshop equipped with advanced machinery—including CNC, milling, laser cutting, bending, and more—we are your trusted partner for all engineering needs, from conceptualization to market-ready production.

Our core services include:

  • Design & Consultation: Guiding you from the earliest stages of idea conceptualization.
  • Simulation & Rapid Prototyping: Verifying your technologies through cutting-edge simulations and prototypes.
  • Customization: Tailoring solutions at both component and system levels to meet your specific requirements.
  • Scale-Up: Transitioning projects seamlessly from the laboratory to industrial scales.
  • Production: Delivering end-to-end manufacturing support for market readiness.

We serve both academic and industrial markets, collaborating with universities, research centers, institutes, multinational corporations (MNCs), and small-to-medium enterprises (SMEs). Our areas of expertise include:

  • Environmental Engineering
  • Precision Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Thermal & Fluid Dynamics
  • Automation & Robotics

With a global presence and an expert engineering team, we are committed to delivering innovative and industry-leading solutions that make a difference.
